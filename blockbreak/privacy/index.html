Block Break Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Block Break does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Local game data

Block Break stores gameplay preferences and progress locally on your device, such as sound settings, tutorial completion, selected background, and best score. This information does not leave your device.

No tracking or analytics

Block Break does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, account login, location data, camera, microphone, contacts, or payment information.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact Dahl Industries AB at info@dahlindustries.se.
