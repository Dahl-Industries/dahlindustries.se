Frequently asked questions

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Osito works 100% offline — every course, lesson, review and the Honey Run game live on your device. Learn on a plane, on the metro, or anywhere with no signal.

Is it really free? Any ads or subscription?

Osito is free to learn, with no ads, no subscription and no account. Nothing blocks your progress.

Which languages can I learn?

Fifteen: Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Polish, Turkish, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic and Hindi. Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, Arabic and Hindi include romanization so you can read and pronounce every word.

How does the review system work?

Osito uses spaced repetition (the SM-2 method) to bring words back right before you'd forget them. Words that are due appear in your Honey Pot — clear it daily and vocabulary moves into long-term memory.

How do I switch language or reset my progress?

Tap the flag / language selector on the home screen to change course. To start over, open Settings and choose Reset progress — all your data lives only on your device.

I can't hear the audio.

Check that your device isn't on silent (the ring/silent switch) and that the volume is up. Audio plays fully offline — no download needed.

Do you collect any of my data?

No — no accounts, no ads, no tracking, no data collection. See the privacy policy.
