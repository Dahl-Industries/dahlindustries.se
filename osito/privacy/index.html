🐻 No data collected

Privacy Policy

Osito · Dahl Industries

Last updated: 7 July 2026

In short

Osito collects no data about you. The app has no accounts, no login, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. Nothing ever leaves your device.

What data is collected?

None. We do not collect, store or transmit any personal or usage data. The app makes no network requests to us or to any third party, and works entirely offline.

Your learning progress

Everything you build in the app — the words you've learned, your review schedule, streaks, honey drops, levels and game scores — is stored only locally on your device. It is available only on that device, is never synced or shared, and is permanently deleted if you remove the app.

Third parties

Osito uses no third-party services, advertising networks or analytics libraries. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

Osito is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). Because no data is collected, no information about children — or anyone else — is processed.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at info@dahlindustries.se.
