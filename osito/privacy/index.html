The policy

Plain and
simple.

The whole thing, in seven short points.

In short

Osito collects no data about you.

The app has no accounts, no login, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. It makes no network requests to us or to any third party, and works entirely offline. Nothing ever leaves your device.

01

What data is collected

None. We do not collect, store or transmit any personal or usage data. There is nothing to opt out of, because nothing is gathered in the first place.

02

Your learning progress

The words you've learned, your review schedule, streaks, honey drops, levels and game scores are stored only locally on your device. They are never synced or shared, and are permanently deleted if you remove the app.

03

Third parties

Osito uses no third-party services, advertising networks or analytics libraries. No data is shared with anyone, because none is collected.

04

Children

Osito is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). Because no data is collected, no information about children — or anyone else — is ever processed.

05

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date at the top.

06

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@dahlindustries.se and a real person replies.
