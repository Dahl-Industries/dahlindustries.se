Why Osito

Learn smart,
remember more.

Built to make words stick — not to sell your data.

01

15 languages

Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Polish, Turkish, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic & Hindi. Romanization on every non-Latin card.

02

Remember every word

Spaced repetition (SM-2) brings words back right before you'd forget them. Due words wait in your Honey Pot for a quick daily review.

03

Practice four ways

Match, choose, type and listen — with natural pronunciation on every card, so each word locks in from every angle.

04

Play to learn

Honey Run — a quick game where you steer Osito into the correct translation. Playing literally counts as review.

05

Build a habit

Collect honey drops, fill honeycombs and keep your streak alive. Small wins that make it easy to come back tomorrow.

06

Fully private

Works 100% offline. No account, no ads, no tracking, no data collection — everything stays on your device.
