01

Head-to-head football

90 seconds, most goals win. One thumb runs, the other jumps and shoots. Time a leaping header and the ball thunders off your skull. A tie? Sudden-death Golden Goal.

02

Six superpowers

Charge the meter, then turn the match: Rocket Shot, Deep Freeze, Big Head, Magnet Boot, The Wall and Ghost Ball. Every hero has a signature.

03

A squad of heroes

Eight original characters — a flame-haired striker, a shieldmaiden, a genius, a 72-year-old cannon, and yes, a moose. Unlock heroes and match-ball skins with coins you earn.

04

Win the Midsummer Cup

An eight-round summer knockout, each rival sharper than the last — from the group stage to the final. Lift the trophy, bank the coins.

05

Online 1v1 & ranked

Real-time head-to-head through Game Center. Play a friendly, or enter ranked and climb the world leaderboard win by win.

06

Fair play, always

No ads. No subscriptions. No tracking. Every hero and skin is earned by playing, and your save lives on your phone.
