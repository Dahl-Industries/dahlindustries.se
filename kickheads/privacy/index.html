Privacy Policy

Kickheads · Dahl Industries AB

Last updated: 7 July 2026

In short

Kickheads collects no personal data about you. There are no accounts of ours, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. Your progress stays on your device. Online 1v1 and leaderboards are provided by Apple Game Center — and the whole game can be played offline against the computer without it.

What data does the app collect?

None directly. We do not collect, store or transmit personal or usage data to ourselves or to any third party. The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics and no tracking.

Your progress

Your coins, unlocked heroes and skins, match statistics and rating are stored only locally on your device. They are available only on the device and are deleted if you remove the app. They are never synced to us or shared.

Online play & Game Center

To play online 1v1 and to appear on leaderboards, Kickheads uses Apple Game Center. If you choose to sign in, Apple handles matchmaking and your Game Center identity, and your rating and career-goal totals are submitted to Game Center leaderboards. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your Game Center account details beyond what Apple provides to run a live match, and we never sell or share them. You can play the entire game offline, without signing in.

Third parties

Apart from Apple Game Center for the optional online features, the app uses no third-party services, ad networks or analytics libraries. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data, no information about children is processed by us.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
