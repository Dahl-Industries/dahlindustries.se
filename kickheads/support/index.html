Frequently asked

How do I play?

Hold the ◀ ▶ arrows to run, tap JUMP to leap and KICK to shoot. Your big head is your best weapon — time a leaping header and the ball flies hardest. Ninety seconds a match, most goals wins; a tie goes to sudden-death Golden Goal.

Is it free? Are there ads?

Kickheads is free with no ads, no subscriptions and no in-app purchases. Every hero and skin is earned by playing.

How does online 1v1 work?

Online matches run in real time through Apple Game Center. Play a friendly, or enter ranked mode where every win climbs the world leaderboard. Sign in to Game Center from the Online screen to get started.

How do I unlock heroes and skins?

Earn coins by playing matches and winning the Midsummer Cup, then spend them in Squad & Skins to unlock new heroes and match-ball skins.

How do I reset my progress?

Your coins, unlocks, stats and rating live only on your device. Removing the app clears all of it — there's no account and nothing stored on our side.

Does the game collect my data?

No — no ads, no tracking, no accounts of ours. Online play and leaderboards use Apple Game Center under Apple's own privacy terms. Read more in the privacy policy.

Kickheads is an independent arcade football game by Dahl Industries AB. Requires iPhone, iOS 17 or later. Online play requires Apple Game Center.
