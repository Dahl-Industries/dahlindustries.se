Frequently asked

How do I play?

Swipe left or right to switch lanes, swipe up to jump the amber ember bars, and swipe down to glide under the pink light-gates. Avoid the red flare walls — one touch ends the run. One thumb, portrait, no menus.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Hyperlane plays fully offline and makes no network requests — ride on a plane, on the subway, anywhere.

How are my high scores saved?

Your best score and distance are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded anywhere, and deleting the app removes them.

How do I reset my best score?

Open Settings from the title screen or pause menu and tap Reset Bests.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

None. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, no tracking. Read more in the privacy policy.

What do I need to play?

An iPhone running iOS 17 or later. If sound or haptics seem off, check the silent switch and the in-game Settings.
