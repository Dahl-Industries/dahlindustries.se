Frequently asked

How do I play?

Swipe left or right to switch lanes, swipe up to jump the amber ember bars, and swipe down to glide under the pink light-gates. Avoid the red flare walls — one touch ends the run. It's one thumb, portrait, no menus.

How are my high scores saved?

Your best score and distance are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app removes them.

Can I reset my best score?

Yes. Open Settings from the title screen or pause menu and tap Reset Bests.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Hyperlane needs no internet connection and makes no network requests — play on a plane, on the subway, anywhere.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

None. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions. The whole game is free.

Do you collect any of my data?

No. Hyperlane has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. See our Privacy Policy.

What do I need to play?

An iPhone running iOS 17 or later.

Sound or haptics not working?

Check that your device isn't in silent mode (Hyperlane respects the silent switch and mixes with your own music), and that Sound and Haptics are enabled in the in-game Settings.
