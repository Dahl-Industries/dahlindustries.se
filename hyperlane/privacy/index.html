Privacy Policy

Hyperlane · Dahl Industries AB

Last updated: 7 July 2026

The short version

Hyperlane collects no data about you. The game has no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking tools. There is no network connection, so nothing ever leaves your device.

What information we collect

None. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data. The game sends no data to us or to any third party.

Your scores and settings

Your best score, best distance, and your sound and haptics preferences are saved only on your device using standard on-device storage. This information is available only on your device and is removed if you delete the app. It is never synced and never shared.

Third parties

Hyperlane uses no third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics libraries. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected, no information about children is processed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at info@dahlindustries.se.
