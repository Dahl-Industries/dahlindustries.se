Hyperlane

Privacy Policy

Last updated 7 July 2026
The short version: Hyperlane collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking. There is no network connection, so nothing about you ever leaves your device.

Information we collect

None. Hyperlane does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not ask you to create an account or sign in, and we do not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking software.

Data stored on your device

Your best score, best distance, and your sound and haptics preferences are saved locally on your iPhone using the operating system's standard on-device storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app permanently removes it.

Network use

Hyperlane makes no network requests and works fully offline. Because nothing is transmitted, there is no data for us — or any third party — to receive.

Children

Hyperlane is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at info@dahlindustries.se.
