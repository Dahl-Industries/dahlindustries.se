HYPERLANE

Chase the aurora. Outrun the light.
A twitch-perfect neon hoverboard runner for iPhone.
Coming to the App Store

Ride a hoverboard through a polar-night world where the whole palette hue-shifts as you speed up — green to blue to magenta to gold. Pure reflex. Instantly readable. Impossibly hard to put down.

How you ride

Carve the lanes

Swipe left and right to cut across three lanes of neon.

Jump & glide

Swipe up to launch the ember bars, down to glide under the light-gates.

Dodge the walls

One touch of a red flare wall and it's a whiteout. Shave them by a hair for near-miss bonuses.

What you chase

Velocity tiers

Speed climbs forever — and every tier repaints the whole sky a new colour.

Aurora shards

Collect shards streaking through the dark. Grab Magnet and Shield power-ups to push further.

A new best

A local high score only you can beat. A cleaner line than the last. Then go again.

Built right

No ads. Ever.

No banners, no interstitials, no in-app purchases. The whole game, free.

No tracking

No accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics. Your best scores stay on your device.

Fully offline

Plays anywhere — subway, plane, off the grid. iPhone, iOS 17 or later.
