Frequently asked questions

Do I need an internet connection to play?

No. Playing the bots, solving puzzles and taking lessons all work fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for online Game Center matches.

How strong are the bots?

There are twelve, rated from 350 (Ebba) to 2300 (Sigrid). Each one is the same engine tuned to a target rating — the weaker bots make honest, human-looking mistakes rather than playing perfectly.

How does my rating work?

You start with a rating based on the experience level you pick during setup. Rated games against bots and online opponents adjust it up or down, just like a real Elo rating. Turn a game's Rated switch off to play casually.

How do I play against friends?

Choose Online on the Play tab and sign in to Game Center. Matches are turn-based, so you can each move whenever it suits you — invite a friend or get matched automatically.

Can I take a move back, or review a finished game?

Yes — offline games support undo, and you can tap any move in the history to step back through the game. Undo is disabled in rated online matches.

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings → Reset progress. Your rating, stats and saved games live only on your device.

Does the app collect my data?

No — no ads, no tracking, no analytics. Online play uses Apple's Game Center; everything else stays on your device. See the privacy policy.
