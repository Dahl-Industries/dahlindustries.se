Privacy Policy

Schack · Dahl Industries AB

Last updated: 7 July 2026

In short

Schack collects no data about you. There are no accounts to create, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. The app is built to be played, not to profile you.

What data is collected?

None. Dahl Industries does not collect, store or transmit any personal or usage data. The app sends no data to us or to any advertising or analytics third party.

Your games and progress

Your saved games, rating, XP, puzzle and lesson progress and settings are stored only locally on your device. They are available only on that device and are removed if you delete the app. They are not synced to us and never shared.

Online play (Game Center)

Online multiplayer and the leaderboard are optional and use Apple's Game Center. If you choose to play online, Apple handles the matchmaking, your Game Center identity and the exchange of moves between players; your rating may be submitted to a Game Center leaderboard. This is provided by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. Dahl Industries does not receive or store this information. If you never use online play, no data leaves your device.

Other third parties

The app uses no advertising networks and no analytics libraries. No data is shared for marketing purposes with anyone.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected, no information about children is processed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Contact

Questions? Email us at info@dahlindustries.se.
