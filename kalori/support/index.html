Frequently asked

How do I log a meal?

Three ways, mix them freely: photograph your plate, say or type what you ate in plain language, or scan a barcode. Kalori estimates the calories and macros and logs it for you.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Kalori runs entirely on your iPhone using Apple’s on-device intelligence, so it works in airplane mode. Only a barcode scan needs the internet — and offline it reads the package with the camera instead.

How do I log a workout, water, or a weigh-in?

Just say or type it — “30 minute run”, “a big glass of water”, “I weigh 78 kg today”. A workout adds its burned calories back to your daily budget.

How is my daily goal set?

A short setup calculates a personalised daily calorie goal from your body, activity level, and whether you want to lose, maintain, or gain. You can change your details anytime and the goal follows.

How do I change the language?

Kalori follows your iPhone’s language automatically and is available in 69 languages — it even replies to you in your own language.

Do you collect my data?

No. Your journal stays on your device, there are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking. Read the full privacy policy.
