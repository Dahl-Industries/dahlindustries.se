Why Kalori

Tracking that
finally sticks.

A food journal that listens — not a database to dig through.

01

Photograph your plate

Point the camera and Kalori reads each food, estimates calories and macros, and logs it — even a packaged snack in poor light.

02

Just say it

Talk or type in plain language. “A chicken caesar salad and a sourdough roll” is logged in seconds. No searching, no forms.

03

Scan any barcode

Aim at a packaged food for instant nutrition facts, then log the exact serving with a single tap.

04

Your day, as a magazine

Every meal becomes a studio photograph in your journal. Zoom out to a catalogue of everything you’ve eaten.

05

The whole picture

Log a workout and Kalori adds the burned calories back to your budget. Track water, weigh-ins, and trends over time.

06

Runs on your iPhone

Works in airplane mode. The intelligence is on-device — no accounts, no ads, no tracking. In 69 languages.
