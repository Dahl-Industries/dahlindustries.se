Frequently asked

How do I play?

Twill jumps automatically — you just steer. Tilt your iPhone left or right, or drag a thumb, to land each bounce on the next platform. Mushroom springs launch you higher, dandelion pads drift, and brittle twigs snap. Dodge or bop the bramble-bugs, and try not to fall.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Skyhop makes no network requests and needs no connection — play on a plane, on the subway, anywhere.

How is my best score saved?

Your best height is stored only on your device. It is never uploaded anywhere and is not tied to any account.

How do I reset my best score?

Your best lives only on your device — deleting and reinstalling Skyhop gives you a fresh start from zero.

How do I turn the sound on or off?

Tap the speaker icon in the corner of the title screen to mute or unmute. Skyhop also respects your phone's silent switch.

The tilt steering isn't working?

Tilt uses your device's motion sensor and only works on a real iPhone. Hold the phone comfortably upright and lean it left or right — or simply drag with a thumb instead, which always works.

Does the app collect my data?

No — no accounts, no analytics, no tracking of any kind. Read more in the privacy policy.

What do I need to play?

An iPhone running iOS 17 or later.
