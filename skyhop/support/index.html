Frequently asked

How do I play?

Twill jumps automatically — you just steer. Tilt your iPhone left or right, or drag a thumb, to land each bounce on the next platform. Mushroom springs launch you higher, dandelion pads drift, and brittle twigs snap. Dodge or bop the bramble-bugs, and try not to fall. It's one hand, portrait, no menus.

How is my best score saved?

Your best height is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded anywhere, and it is not tied to any account.

How do I reset my best score?

Your best lives only on your device. Deleting and reinstalling Skyhop gives you a fresh start from zero.

How do I turn the sound on or off?

Tap the speaker icon in the top corner of the title screen to mute or unmute. Your choice is remembered. Skyhop also respects your phone's silent switch.

The tilt steering isn't working?

Tilt uses your device's motion sensor and only works on a real iPhone (not the Simulator). Hold the phone comfortably upright and lean it left or right — or simply drag with a thumb instead, which always works.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Skyhop needs no internet connection and makes no network requests — play on a plane, on the subway, anywhere.

Are there ads or in-app purchases?

None. No ads, no in-app purchases, no subscriptions.

Do you collect any of my data?

No. Skyhop has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking of any kind. See our Privacy Policy.

What do I need to play?

An iPhone running iOS 17 or later.
