Privacy Policy

Skyhop · Dahl Industries AB

Last updated: 7 July 2026

In short

Skyhop collects no data about you. The app has no accounts, no login, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. There is no network connection, so nothing ever leaves your device.

What data is collected?

None. We do not collect, store or transmit any personal or usage data. The app sends no data to us or to any third party.

Data stored on your device

Your best height and your sound preference are saved only locally on your device using the operating system's standard on-device storage. This information is available only on your device, is never synced or shared, and is deleted if you remove the app.

Motion & sensors

Skyhop reads your device's motion sensor so you can tilt to steer. This happens entirely on your device, in the moment of play. Motion data is used only to move the character and is never recorded, stored or transmitted.

Third parties

The app uses no third-party services, ad networks or analytics libraries. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected, no information about children is processed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
