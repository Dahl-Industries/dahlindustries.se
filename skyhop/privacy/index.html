Skyhop

Privacy Policy

Last updated 7 July 2026
The short version: Skyhop collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking. There is no network connection, so nothing about you ever leaves your device.

Information we collect

None. Skyhop does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not ask you to create an account or sign in, and we do not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking software.

Data stored on your device

Your best height and your sound preference are saved locally on your iPhone using the operating system's standard on-device storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app permanently removes it.

Motion & sensors

Skyhop reads your device's motion sensor so you can tilt to steer. This happens entirely on your device in the moment of play. Motion data is used only to move the character and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Network use

Skyhop makes no network requests and works fully offline. Because nothing is transmitted, there is no data for us — or any third party — to receive.

Children

Skyhop is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.
