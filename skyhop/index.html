Why Skyhop

Easy to start,
hard to stop.

Built for a calm minute — or one more best-beating run.

01

Tilt to steer

Twill hops on their own — you just steer. Tilt your iPhone or drag a thumb to land each bounce on the next platform.

02

A tower of surprises

Leaf pads, mushroom springs that launch you sky-high, drifting dandelion seeds, and brittle twigs that snap under your feet.

03

Dodge the bugs

Bramble-bugs creep across your path as you climb. Slip past them — or bop one from above for a little boost.

04

The sky opens up

The world cools from soft morning mist into clear high blue, and the distant canopy falls away below you.

05

Chase your best

Every metre pushes your best height further. A personal record only you can beat — then hop again.

06

Calm & private

Fully offline. No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no data collection. Everything stays on your device.
