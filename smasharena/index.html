Why Smash Arena

Pick up.
One more run.

Built to feel good in your thumb — not to sell your data.

01

One-touch control

Drag anywhere to move — your paddle follows your finger and swings itself on contact. Flick through the ball to smash and bend the arc with spin.

02

Arcade ladder

First to 11, win by 2, clears the round. Every rival that loads is faster, sharper and less forgiving than the last.

03

Real-time 1v1

Challenge a friend on another iPhone over Game Center and rally head-to-head, live. Whoever blinks first drops the ball.

04

Global rankings

Game Center leaderboards for your best run and your longest streak. Climb the board, not just the ladder.

05

Built to feel

Tactile haptics on every hit, an original arcade soundtrack and a locked 60fps. Solo play works fully offline.

06

Totally private

No data collected. No ads, no tracking, no accounts. Everything stays on your device.
