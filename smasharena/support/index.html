Frequently asked

How do I play?

Drag anywhere on the screen to move your paddle — it swings itself on contact. Flick quickly through the ball to smash it and add spin. First to 11 points, win by 2, clears the round and loads a tougher rival.

Do I need an internet connection?

No — Smash Arena is completely offline. Every rally, round and high score runs on your device, and no connection is ever needed.

How do I turn sound or haptics off?

Open Settings from the title screen, or the pause menu during a solo match. Sound also respects your device's silent switch.

How do I reset my high scores?

Go to Settings → Reset Records. Your high score, best streak and best rally are stored only on your device, and deleting the app clears them too.

Does the app collect my data?

No — no data is collected, no ads, no tracking. Read more in the privacy policy.
