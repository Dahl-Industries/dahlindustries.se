Smash Arena: Table Tennis Game — iPhone, iOS 17 or later

Smash Arena is a fast, minimalist 3D table tennis game with a retro-arcade soul. Rally, smash, and climb the ladder — every round you clear loads a tougher opponent.

How to play

Drag anywhere on the screen to move your paddle — it follows your finger and swings automatically on contact. Moving the paddle quickly at the moment of contact aims the return, adds power, and puts spin on the ball. First to 11 points (win by 2) clears the round.

Sound & haptics

Both can be toggled in Settings on the title screen, or directly from the pause menu during a match. Sound respects your device's silent switch.

Versus & leaderboards

VS Match pits you against another player on their own iPhone in real time, and Rankings shows the global high-score and best-streak leaderboards. Both use Apple's Game Center — if the buttons ask you to sign in, enable Game Center in iOS Settings. An internet connection is required for versus matches; solo play works fully offline.

High scores

Your high score, best streak, and best rally are stored locally on your device. You can reset them under Settings → Reset Records. Deleting the app also deletes all saved data — nothing is stored anywhere else.

Something not working?

Email us and we'll get back to you. Include your iPhone model and iOS version if you can — it helps us reproduce the issue.
