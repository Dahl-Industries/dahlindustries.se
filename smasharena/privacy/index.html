Smash Arena Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Smash Arena does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Local game data

Smash Arena stores gameplay preferences and progress locally on your device, such as sound and haptics settings, high score, best streak, and best rally. This information does not leave your device, and deleting the app removes it entirely.

No tracking or analytics

Smash Arena does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking, account login, location data, camera, microphone, contacts, or payment information. The app never connects to the internet.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact Dahl Industries AB at info@dahlindustries.se.
