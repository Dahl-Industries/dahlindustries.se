Privacy Policy

Smash Arena · Dahl Industries AB

Last updated: 9 July 2026

In short

Smash Arena collects no data about you. The game has no accounts, no login, no ads, no analytics and no tracking. It is fully offline — nothing ever leaves your device.

What data is collected?

None. We do not collect, store or transmit any personal or usage data. The game never connects to the internet and sends no data to us or to any third party.

Your game progress

Your high score, best streak, best rally and your sound and haptics settings are stored only locally on your device. They are available only on the device and are deleted if you remove the game. They are never synced to us or shared.

Third parties

The game uses no third-party services, analytics, advertising networks or tracking libraries. No data is shared with anyone.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected, no information about children is processed.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.

Questions? Email us at info@dahlindustries.se.
